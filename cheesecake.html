Cheesecake

Indulge your sweet tooth with our creamy and decadent cheesecake recipe. With a buttery graham cracker crust and a luscious cream cheese filling, this dessert is a timeless favorite that never fails to impress. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a slice of heaven, my easy-to-follow recipe will guide you to cheesecake perfection.

Ingredients:

  • Graham crackers
  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sour cream
  • Lemon juice(optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Crush the graham crackers into fine crumbs. Mix with melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan to form the crust.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Stir in vanilla extract and sour cream until fully incorporated. Add lemon zest if desired for a hint of brightness.
  7. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the prepared crust, spreading it evenly.
  8. Tap the pan gently on the counter to release any air bubbles.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
  10. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside with the door ajar for about an hour.
  11. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to chill and set.
  12. Once chilled, carefully remove the cheesecake from the pan and transfer to a serving plate.
  13. Slice and serve with your favorite toppings, such as fresh berries or fruit compote.
  14. Enjoy every creamy bite of this irresistible dessert!
