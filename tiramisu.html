Tiramisu

Indulge in the classic Italian dessert with our irresistible Tiramisu recipe. Layers of delicate ladyfingers soaked in coffee and liqueur, nestled between rich mascarpone cream and dusted with cocoa powder, create a symphony of flavors and textures that will transport you to the streets of Rome with every bite. Follow my step-by-step instructions and tips to whip up this elegant dessert that's sure to impress your friends and family.


Ingredients:

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs
  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Coffee
  • Liquer (optional)
  • Cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the coffee and let it cool to room temperature. Add liqueur if desired.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine mascarpone cheese, egg yolks, and sugar. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture briefly, ensuring they are soaked but not soggy.
  5. Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish.
  6. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mascarpone cream on top.
  8. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.
  9. Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder for an elegant finish.
  10. Slice and serve chilled. Buon Appetito!
